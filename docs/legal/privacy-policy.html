Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Herblore ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. Apple's "Hide My Email" feature is fully supported.

User Data: Your health goals, supplement stack, daily intake logs, and preferences are stored to provide personalized recommendations. This data is stored securely in our database hosted in the EU (Frankfurt).

AI Coach Conversations: If you use the AI Coach feature, your questions and the coach's responses are stored to provide conversation context. These are linked to your account and not shared with third parties.

We do NOT collect:

  • Health data from Apple Health or HealthKit
  • Location data
  • Device contacts or photos
  • Advertising identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized supplement recommendations and daily schedules
  • Generate interaction warnings between supplements in your stack
  • Power the AI Coach feature with context about your stack and goals
  • Manage your subscription
  • Improve the app experience

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany), in compliance with GDPR data residency requirements. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Authentication is handled through Supabase Auth with Apple Sign-In integration.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider (EU-hosted)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (processes only purchase receipts)
  • Anthropic: AI Coach queries are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Queries include your message and anonymized context (goals, stack names) but not your identity
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or valid legal process

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@herblore.app.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. AI Coach conversation history is automatically deleted after 90 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Herblore is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@herblore.app
